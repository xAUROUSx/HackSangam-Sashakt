AQI

    The Air Quality Index (AQI) is used for reporting daily air 
    quality.It tells you how clean or polluted your air is,and 
    what associated health effects z might be a concernfor you .
    The AQI focuses on health effectsyou may experience within a
     few hours or days after breathing polluted air.
